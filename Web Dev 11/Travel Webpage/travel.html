Baguio Vacation

the story

We planned a very unexpected trip... Clue: It is known as the City of Pines, bestowed upon it because it is perched in the middle of tropical pine forests around 1500 meters above sea level, ensuring cool, pleasant temperatures year-round. Yes!!! You guessed it right -- the Summer Capital of the Philippines -- Baguio! Over a bowl of Ramen, our friend group decided to impulsively plan a vacation to Baguio over the Christmas break. I don't know what's going to happen, all I know is I'm excited to feel the cold breeze and see the fireworks in the New Year. Thankfully, we have a very organized friend who created a whole Google Sheet for our itinerary which I will share in a while.
