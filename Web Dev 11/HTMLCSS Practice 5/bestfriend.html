My Best Friend

Tabular Stylist Poem

First Stanza

Best friends are angels/ That God Sent along.

They always stay beside you/Whenever things go wrong.

Second Stanza

  1. I'm glad that God blessed me.
  2. With a good friend such as you.
  3. A person to be there.
  4. A person to get me through.

Third Stanza

  • Never turn away from me.
  • I can't bear to be apart.
  • All the pain and suffering.
  • Will be too much for my heart.

Fourth Stanza

You are my best friend, / The one who brightens my day.

The one who cheers me up. / The one who's here to stay.
